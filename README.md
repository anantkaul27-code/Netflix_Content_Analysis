# Netflix Content Analysis

## Overview

In this project, I analyze the Netflix Titles dataset through Python and pandas to uncover trends in Netflix's content library.

The analysis therefore explores the distribution of movies and TV shows, leading content-producing countries, content ratings, and the most common genres.

This project was created as a part of my data analytics portfolio in preperation for graduate studies in Business Analytics.

---

## Dataset

Dataset: Netflix Titles Dataset

The dataset contains information including:

- Movie/TV Show type
- Country
- Release year
- Rating
- Genre
- Director

---

## Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

---

## Questions Explored

- How many Movies vs TV Shows are available?
- Which countries produce the most Netflix content?
- What are the most common content ratings?
- What genres appear most frequently?

---

## Visualizations

### Movies vs TV Shows

Shows the distribution of Movies and TV Shows in the Netflix catalog.

### Top 10 Countries Producing Netflix Content

Highlights the countries contributing the most titles.

### Content Ratings Distribution

Displays the most common audience ratings.

### Top 10 Netflix Genres

Shows the most frequently occurring genres in the dataset.

---

## Key Findings

- Movies greatly outnumber TV Shows.
- The United States contributes the largest number of titles.
- TV-MA is the most common content rating.
- Drama-related genres dominate the Netflix catalog.
